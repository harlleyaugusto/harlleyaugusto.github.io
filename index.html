About me

I hold a Master's degree in Computer Science from Federal University of Minas Gerais, which is ranked as one of the best Brazilian universities in computer science. I have worked with information retrieval, bibliometric indicators and data analysis in my previous research and published two international papers in JCDL and one international journal in Scientometrics. Therefore, my main research interests are Information Retrieval, Digital Libraries, Machine Learning and Data Analysis. Also, I have worked as a temporary professor for three years in Brazilian Universities.

Feb 2013 - May 2013

During my Master's I had the opportunity to do a Internship in Singapore in which I worked with Zhaoxia Wang. Our research wanted to identify the most influent users in a specific subject from a local newspapper named The Stratis Time. Therefore, I developed a crawler to collect data from this newspapper and then analysed the comments. We also studied the user's opinions from Twitter related to their satisfaction with public services as subway and public housing.

Technologies: R, Java, Hibernate, Maven, MySQL and Selenium.
